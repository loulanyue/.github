# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project spaces should be respectful, technically constructive, and welcoming to people
with different experience levels and backgrounds. Good participation includes clear
feedback, patience, attribution, respect for privacy, and a focus on the work rather
than the person.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, doxxing, sexualized
content, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when behavior harms the community. Report conduct concerns privately to
`@loulanyue` using a contact method on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Reports will be
handled as confidentially and fairly as practical.

This code applies in repository spaces and when someone is publicly representing a
project maintained by `@loulanyue`.
